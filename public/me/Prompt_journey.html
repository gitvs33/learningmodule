The Journey of a Prompt

From Your Words to the Model's Response

How do you learn?

1. YOUR PROMPT

The process starts with the raw text you provide.

→

2. TOKENIZATION

The text is broken down into smaller pieces called tokens.

→

3. EMBEDDING

Each token is converted into a numerical vector that represents its meaning.

→

4. NEURAL NETWORK

The vectors are processed by the network (e.g., a Transformer) to understand context and relationships.

→

5. DECODING

The network's output vectors are converted back into tokens.

→

6. FINAL RESPONSE

The tokens are assembled into the final text response.
